#  ProCredit Holding: Financial Performance Analytics

## Project Overview
This project presents a financial analysis of ProCredit Holding (2021 - Q3 2025). It tracks key performance indicators (KPIs) to evaluate profitability and operational efficiency.

## Key Results for North Macedonia (Sep-2025)
* **ROE (Return on Equity):** 10.2%
* **NIM (Net Interest Margin):** 2.7%
* **CIR (Cost-Income Ratio):** 59.2%
* **NPL Ratio:** 1.5% (Low risk)
* 
## Risk Management & ICAAP Framework Integration
This analysis is designed with the **Internal Capital Adequacy Assessment Process (ICAAP)** principles in mind, focusing on Pillar 2 risk monitoring:

* **Credit Risk Assessment:** Monitoring the **NPL Ratio at 1.5%** to ensure asset quality remains within the bank's risk appetite.
* **Capital Planning:** Utilizing the **10.2% ROE** as a primary indicator for internal capital generation capacity.
* **Interest Rate Risk (IRRBB):** Tracking **Net Interest Margin (NIM) at 2.7%** to evaluate the impact of market fluctuations on the bank's economic value.
* **Operational Risk:** Analyzing the **Cost-Income Ratio (59.2%)** as a proxy for operational efficiency and resilience.
